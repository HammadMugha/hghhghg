Company Profile

Technodent Dental Solution (TDS) Pvt Ltd group recognized for quality products & services as well as established name in Indian market place, offers widest range of Indian & International quality Dental Equipments. The range includes Dental Chair Units, CBCT & OPG Machine, Lasers, Scalers, AERB approved X-Ray Machine, Intra Oral Cameras, Autoclaves, Digital Radiography Sensors, Handpiece range, Dental Microscopes, Light Cure Units, Bleaching Units, Physio Dispenser, Compressor, Suctions, Intraoral Scanner.
